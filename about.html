About Us

Welcome to Ruban Liner Upholstery Work. We are a locally owned and operated business in Madurai with over 7 years of experience in the upholstery industry.

We specialize in breathing new life into your old furniture. Whether it's a cherished sofa set that has lost its comfort or a mattress that needs a new cover, our skilled craftsmanship ensures premium quality results.

Why Choose Us?

  • Local Expertise: We know the needs of Madurai households and businesses.
  • Quality Materials: We use only high-density foams and durable fabrics/rexine.
  • Affordable Pricing: Top-notch service at competitive local rates.
  • Doorstep Service: For major works, we can visit your location for inspection.

About the Founder

Ruban Liner Upholstery Work operates under the guidance of experienced professionals who are passionate about furniture restoration. Our goal is to provide 100% customer satisfaction.
